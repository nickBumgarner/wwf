About the Winter WonderFest

Winter WonderFEST is proudly produced by a newly established nonprofit organization, Festival of Cheer, Inc. Our organization has created this festival to provide greater cultural activities for families to enjoy during the holiday season. Most importantly, Winter WonderFEST will provide critically needed funding to the various nonprofits that serve the residents and visitors of Sussex County, Delaware.
